Report an issue

When you run into issues in MARqeT, report these as quickly as possible. Consult the Support decision tree on the dashboard for general guidance.
  1. In the left blue menu, click Dashboard.
  2. In the left tile, go to the Support section and click Report an application issue.
  3. Fill in the form as completely as possible.
    Tip: Click for more information about Priorities and formatting of your Description.
  4. Click Submit.
